**Movement**

Press A to turn left
Press D to turn right
Press W to turn front
Press S to turn back
--------------------------
Press Q to walk front

**Camera**
Press I to zoom in 
Press O to zoom out
Press P to change viewport

**Hand**
Press 7 to move the left arm up or down

**Leg**
Press L to move the legs

**Scene**
press 1 or 2 to change to day or night
press 3 to turn light on or off

**Weapon**
press z to equip a sword on right hand 
press x to equip a spike hammer on left hand

**Other**
Press spacebar to back to original view and position
Press T to toggle texture on or off
