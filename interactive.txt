**Movement**

Press A to turn left
Press D to turn right
Press W to turn front
Press S to turn back
--------------------------
Press Q to walk front

**Camera**
Press I to zoom in 
Press O to zoom out

**Hand**
Press H to move the hands

**Leg**
Press L to move the legs

**Scene**
press 1 or 2 to change to day or night

**Body Color**
press 3 to change the steel texture of jaeger
press 4 to change the stone texture of jaeger
press 5 to change the platinum texture of jaeger
press 6 to change the spiky texture of jaeger

**Weapon**
press z to equip a sword on right hand 
press x to equip a spike hammer on left hand
